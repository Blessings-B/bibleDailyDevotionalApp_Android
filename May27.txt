Living A Healthy Life
'So, whether you eat or drink or whatever you do, do it all for the glory of God.'Â 
1 Corinthians 10:31

Text: 1 Corinthians 10:23-33

Your life should be lived to the glory of God and your health should also glorify Him. 5 excellent healthy habits that you can adopt are as follows:
>Eat more fruits and vegetables like watermelon, oranges, carrots and less of processed foods like bread, cakes, noodles etc.
>Get physically active. Start an exercise plan, stay consistent and share this healthy routine with your friends and family!
>Keep your clothes and your environment clean.
>Listen to your body. Have regular check-ups and do not ignore aches and pains. Get seen by a medical professional as soon as possible.
>Be positive, cast your cares on God (Psalm 55:22), and practice being thankful (Philippians 4:8).
Jesus paid a high price for your life, and He wants you to live life to the fullest. So, put aside unhealthy habits today!

FURTHER STUDY: John 10:10; Romans 12:2
PRAYER: Lord, bless my plans to live healthily and help me to be committed to it, in Jesus name, Amen
TIP OF THE DAY: Connect with people and look out for things that will inspire you and motivate you to stay healthy.
